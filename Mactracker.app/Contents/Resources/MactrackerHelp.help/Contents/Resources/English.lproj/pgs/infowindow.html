Looking up model details

The info windows are the repository of all of Mactracker's information. They do, of course, vary in configuration based on what model you are looking at.

To look up model details:

  1. Choose a category from the main window.

  2. Locate the model you wish to view in the list on the right.

  3. Double-click to view the model's technical specifications.

  4. Use the info window tabs to view the various specifications.

Tips

  • Clicking on the model's icon will play the startup chime. Holding down the option key when you click on the model's icon will play the death chime.

  • Click on the Processor Speed to gauge the real-world performance with scores courtesy of Primate Labs Geekbench. The included results reflect an average of the user submitted benchmarks for the particular model.

  • Initial Price can show the model's original price in Australian dollars, Canadian dollars, euros, Hong Kong dollars, Japanese yen, New Zealand dollars, Norwegian krone, pounds sterling, Renminbi, Swedish kronor, Swiss francs and United States dollars. Choose your preferred currency in Preferences.

  • Current Price can show the model's current estimated value in euros, pounds sterling, or United States dollars. Current Price is determined by analyzing eBay auctions over the last 6 months.

  • The Notes tab allows you to add your own comments as well as links to Internet addresses, files, and folders that relate to the selected model.
