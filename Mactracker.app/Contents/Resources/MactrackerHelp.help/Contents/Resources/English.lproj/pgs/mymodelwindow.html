Keep track of your own Apple hardware

With "My Models" you can add details about your Apple products, including their serial number, purchase date, and warranty coverage end date.

To add details about your Apple product:

  1. Choose the My Models category from the main window.

  2. Click the Add (+) button at the bottom of the main window.

  3. In the next window, you can enter the details about your Apple product.

  4. Click the Change button to select the type of model you have added.
  5. Click the Save button after you have selected the type of model.
  6. Click Save Changes when you are finished adding details.

Tips

  • Including a date in Warranty Ends allows you to see all your products that are in warranty, about to leave warranty in the next 30 days, and those that are outside their warranty.

  • You can add a reminder to Calendar or Reminders to notify you that a model's warranty is about to expire. This option can be enabled in Preferences.

  • You can import a system profile to create a new My Model entry. The system profile needs to be created by Apple System Profiler version 10.5.7 or later.
